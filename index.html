Mengmeng Kuang

My name is Mengmeng Kuang, an Applied Researcher (NLP Algorithm) at Tencent (WeChat Group) currently. Before that, I received the M.Phil degree in Computer Science from The University of Hong Kong. I am fortunate to be supervised by Dr. Hing-fung Ting, who taught me how to do research and write papers. Prior to HKU, I received the B.Eng degree in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin China, supervised by Prof. Tiejun Zhao. During that period, I learned a lot of basic knowledge about computer science and cultivated my interest in it.
